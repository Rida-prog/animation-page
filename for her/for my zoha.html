Tulips Were Always You
Every single time I see a tulip — every single time — my mind goes straight to you. Not to a garden, not to a season, not to a photograph. Just you, Zoha. Your face, your smile, your beauty. Tulips remind me of you because they are delicate and bold all at once, breathtakingly beautiful without even trying. Just like you walk into a room and it changes. Just like the way you exist makes everything around you softer and more alive. These 17 tulips are not just flowers — they are every thought of you I carry silently, every moment your beauty made me catch my breath, every prayer I whisper that the world gets to see someone as magnificent as Zoha. You are my tulip girl. You always will be.
✦  "Every tulip I ever see will forever be named after you."
